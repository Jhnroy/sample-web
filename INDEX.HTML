About Us

You can visit one of our three branches in place of Daet, Mercedes, or Labo.

ResaTea is a milk tea shop that strives to offer its costumer enticing beverages at fair and reasonable prices. The term "milk tea" can be used to describe a wide range of teas and tea-based beverages. The most basic definition of milk tea is a tea that contains milk. Our shop sells gratifying, savory, and high-quality milk tea in upgraded flavors. Accordingly, we provide a wide range of beverages, such as fruity yogurts, cheesecake and Oreo series, vodka cocktails, and iced tea. Our drinks are all about budget and quality. It is what it has always been and always will be.
